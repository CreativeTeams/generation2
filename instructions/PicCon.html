Picture Construction Test



This test is minutes long.

The aim of this test is to place the shape, and use it to create a drawing which nobody else will think of. Try to add detail to make the story as complete as possible. Give your drawing a title which helps to further elaborate the story you are drawing.

At the start of this test only one of the participants will be able to move/rotate the shape, you must work together to place it in the best location for your drawing. Once you are happy with it, click place shape and you can start drawing.

You can keep drawing, and adding the title until the time limit is up.
