Idea Generation Test

Your team has been asked by a manufacturer of sports and fitness products to identify new concept products.
The manufacturer is particularly interested in products likely to be appealing to students.
These products might be solutions to unmet needs or improved solutions to existing needs.

They would like you to suggest as many new products as possible, with a description of what the new product is and how it might work.

Ideas will be judged independently based on their potential impact.
