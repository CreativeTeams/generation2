Design Challenge Test



This test is minutes long.

An outdoor equipment company wants to release a new portable chair, suitable for hikers, tourists or attending music festivals, which is easily mobile but that can withstand the British weather.

They would like you to create an initial design.
Please draw your chair and add descriptions to supplement your design.
Use as many pages as you like to provide as much detail and information about the chair as possible.
The company specifically want this chair to be the most innovative in the market - they would like you to design a portable chair that nobody else would think of.
