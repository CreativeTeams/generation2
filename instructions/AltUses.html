Alternative Uses Test

This test is minutes long.

You are all familiar with cardboard boxes.
Please think of as many different uses for them as possible.
Do not limit yourself to any particular size of box, or number of boxes.
Try to think of unusual uses for them, not just standard things that they are used for.

On the next page you will be able to enter your possible uses into one team list.

Remember you are trying to list as many unusual uses as possible. Every idea counts!
